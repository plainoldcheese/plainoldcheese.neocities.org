22-10-2023

getting back into updating this site has made me really happy, I've been thinking about how I spend my time on the internet. I've gone both ways in the past. Absolute off-grid no social media whatsoever, and I've tried to cultivate a presence on the different platforms like Instagram, Facebook, Reddit, etc. Now I think I'm just going to decide what I actually like.

Little bit of a rant Privacy concerns aside I still like Reddit, it feels like it really connects people around interests and it's quite as self centred. Instagram and Facebook have just become so boring and homogenised it doesn't even show you what you follow, just what it thinks you want to see. I don't feel like there's any personal connection there anymore. Mastodon has been okay but finding people I want to follow is difficult. I haven't been on Twitter in years and I'm not sure I want to make any new social accounts I enjoy Discord for a similar reason to Reddit only it feels more personal. I like being able to chat to people about a common interest and discord is great for that. Then lastly there's Neocities. I haven't had the pleasure of any direct contact with any Neocitizens, but I like the vibe, and I'd love to setup a guestbook or something. I think what I really miss is that sense of connection social media used to provide. It used to be about reconnecting with old friends or keeping up to date with what your friends were up to when you weren't together. Now it's very commercialised, there's no sense of time because nothing is chronological and I feel like there's constantly some kind of message being pushed on me. Like the powers at be are trying to manipulate the population. YouTube isn't really social media, but I've decided I'm gonna cut YouTube shorts out. They really make me upset and I can end up doom-scrolling through stuff that I don't like for hours. Short form video feels really dystopian at times. But anyway, that's my take on it and I hope I can be more mindful and curate my online experience in a way that brings me joy and adds to my life.
