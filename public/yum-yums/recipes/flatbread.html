Soft Mediterranean Flatbread

Quick and easy flatbread recipe. Ideal for dipping into hummus or tzatziki, or just as a side.

makes 8 medium sized flatbreads

  • prep time: 15min
  • wait time: 20min
  • cook time: 30 sec each

Ingredients

  • 2 cups bread flour
  • 1 cup lukewarm water
  • 2 tsp salt
  • 1 packet (9 g) instant yeast
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • fresh chopped garlic (optional)
  • extra virgin olive oil (optional)
  • fresh coriander leaves (optional)

Directions

  1. Mix together the water, salt, sugar and instant yeast till frothy (I like to use a tiny whisk).
  2. Add the flour to a bowl.
  3. Add the wet ingredients to the dry ingredients and mix to combine with hands or a fork.
  4. Once the mixture has been combined, knead for 5 minutes or until it forms a smooth ball that springs back a little bit when pressed. If the dough is too dry add a tablespoon of warm water at a time while kneading, if it's too wet add a tbsp of flour.
  5. Cover the bowl with a plastic wrap or a damp towel and let rise for 20 minutes, or until doubled in size
  6. Cut the dough into 8 pieces, and roll into individual balls. Let rest for around 5 minutes.
  7. On a flour dusted surface, roll out the balls into flatbreads. If they keep springing back while rolling, let them rest a little longer.

  9. Optionally, Brush the flatbreads with extra virgin olive oil, and sprinkle with chopped garlic and coriander leaves.
